Survey Synthetic Data

Generate AI answers for survey respondents and export them — live from the database

1 Pick a survey

2 Filter respondents

Only submitted, non-excluded respondents are included. Optionally narrow by submit date.

3 Choose questions

Generate questions the AI can confidently answer from existing responses, and/or write your own. Tick the ones to include. Click “Generate with AI” again to add more.

4 Preview — sample of 10

5 Generate for all respondents

Runs the included questions across the filtered cohort (capped for cost) and produces a raw-data Excel: every respondent with their real answers + the AI-generated answers.
